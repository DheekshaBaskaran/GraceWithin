Support & Recovery Resources

Comprehensive resources for eating disorder support, recovery, and mental health education

National Eating Disorders Association (NEDA) ↗

Comprehensive support, screening tools, and helplines for those affected by eating disorders.

Eating Disorder Hope ↗

Educational resources, recovery stories, and treatment provider listings.

Project HEAL ↗

Equity-focused eating disorder treatment support including financial aid and mentorship programs.

National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH) ↗

Scientific insights and statistics about eating disorders and mental health research.

Body Positive ↗

Promotes body acceptance, intuitive eating, and self-care through education and activism.

ANAD (National Association of Anorexia Nervosa and Associated Disorders) ↗

Free peer support services, mentorship programs, and virtual support groups for those affected by eating disorders.

F.E.A.S.T. (Families Empowered and Supporting Treatment of Eating Disorders) ↗

Provides support, education, and resources for families and caregivers of individuals with eating disorders.
